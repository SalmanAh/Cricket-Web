How Power Bar Works:
-------------------
1. Power bar divided into segments proportional to probabilities
2. Slider moves continuously from left (0) to right (1)
3. Player clicks to stop slider
4. Outcome determined by which segment contains slider position
5. Example: Slider at 0.32 in Aggressive mode = Wicket (0.00-0.40 range)

NOT RANDOM - Outcome strictly depends on slider position!

================================================================================

Animations Implemented:
----------------------
1. Bowling Animation
   - Ball travels from right to batsman
   - Duration: 1.5 seconds
   - CSS keyframes with ease-in timing

2. Batting Animation
   - Bat swings from -45° to 20° and back
   - Duration: 0.5 seconds
   - Triggered on shot

3. Wicket Animation
   - Batsman rotates 90° and falls
   - Opacity reduces
   - Duration: 0.5 seconds

4. Result Display
   - Text scales with "pop" effect
   - Shows outcome (runs or wicket)
   - Color-coded (red=wicket, gold=boundary, green=runs)

5. Slider Movement
   - RequestAnimationFrame for smooth 60fps
   - Completes cycle in 2 seconds
   - Continuous loop until clicked
